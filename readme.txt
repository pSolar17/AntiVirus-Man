# --------------------- #

Controls:

Z - shoot/accept
X - cancel
Arrows - movement
F4 - fullscreen
F5 - toggle CRT overlay
Esc - quit game(it autosaves)
Numpad +/Page Up - Volume UP
Numpad -/Page Down - Volume DOWN
Numpad Enter/Home - Volume MAX
M - Mute/Unmute

# --------------------- #

How to play:

Defeat enemies
Don't die 4Head
Grow your virus strains in lab and become more powerful(hopefully)
